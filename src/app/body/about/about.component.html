About Me

Jaime Moncayo is a Senior Software Engineer with extensive experience in developing high-performance, real-time exchange platforms and ad-tech solutions. Currently a Senior Software Engineer I at Kargo, he leads cross-functional special projects and designs advanced bidding strategies using Go. His previous leadership roles include serving as a Technical Lead at TripleLift, where he managed core auction platforms handling over 18,000 requests per second and integrated machine learning models to optimize pricing. Jaime's technical expertise spans a wide range of languages, including Java, Go, and Python, as well as frameworks like Spring Boot, Angular, and React. He holds a Bachelor of Business Administration in Finance from the University of North Texas and has a professional background that also includes software engineering and financial analysis roles at Fidelity Investments.
